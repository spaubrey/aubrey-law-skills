# Trust Funding Instructions — Drafting Guide

Load this reference when drafting the Funding Instructions & Checklist.

Source guide: `/mnt/project/funding-instructions-guide.md`

---

## Document Identity

- **File name:** `YYYY-MM-DD_Funding-Instructions_[CLIENT-LAST-NAME].docx`
- **Page size:** US Letter (12240 × 15840 DXA)
- **Margins:** Top 1080, Bottom 1080, Left 1008, Right 1008 DXA
- **Default font:** Garamond, 12pt (24 half-points), color `000000` / `2C2C2A`
- **Header margin:** 576 DXA; **Footer margin:** 432 DXA

## Sections to Include (in order)

1. **Why Funding Your Trust Matters**
   — Standard boilerplate about retitling and beneficiary designations.
   — Callout box: "Important: Funding your Trust is your responsibility..."
   — Read full text from `/mnt/project/funding-instructions-guide.md` Section 1.

2. **Before You Begin**
   — Correct titling format block (bold/italic) with `[TRUSTEE NAME(S)]`
   — Certificate of Trust explanation
   — Tax ID / SSN explanation
   — Fill `[TRUST NAME]`, `[DATE OF TRUST]`, `[TRUSTEE NAME(S)]` from intake.

3. **Trust Funding Checklist by Asset Type** (3-column table)
   — Column headers: ☐ | Asset Type | Action Required
   — Header row: navy `1B3A6B` background, white text
   — See full row list in the source guide.
   — **Row suppression:** Remove rows for asset types client does not have
     (out-of-state real estate, business interests, stock options, oil/gas,
     529 plans) based on asset profile from intake.

4. **Retirement Accounts — Special Rules & RMDs**
   — Standard boilerplate about beneficiary designations.
   — Warning callout box about naming trust as primary beneficiary.
   — RMD starting ages table (4 rows).
   — Read full text from source guide Section 4.

5. **What to Save — Your Trust Portfolio**
   — 2-column table: Asset | Document to Retain
   — Read full text from source guide Section 5.

6. **Keeping Your Trust Current**
   — Life events bullet list.
   — Tip callout box.
   — Read from source guide Section 6.

7. **Frequently Asked Questions**
   — 10 Q&A blocks.
   — Read verbatim from source guide Section 7.

8. **Disclaimer** (required, bottom of document)
   — Italic, 10pt, standard legal disclaimer.
   — Read from source guide.

## Callout Box Styling

Use a bordered/shaded paragraph block:
- Light gray background: `F2F2F2`
- Border: single, navy `1B3A6B`, size 6
- Text: italic, Garamond 12pt
- Padding: cell margins top/bottom 80, left/right 120 (DXA)

## Trustee Name Format in Titling Block

Fill Section 2 "Correct Titling Format" block with correct trustee language:

- Joint, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] and [SPOUSE FULL NAME], as Trustee(s), or their successor(s)
  in interest, of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto.`

- Individual, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME], as Trustee, or their successor in interest, of the
  [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto.`

- Add co-trustee name if applicable.
