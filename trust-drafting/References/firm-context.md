## Document Standards

### Legal Instruments (Trust, Pour-Over Will, Assignment of PP, Certificate of Trust)
- Garamond font throughout — 12pt body, 14pt bold titles
- **Black `000000` only** — no navy, teal, gray, or any accent colors
- **No logo, no header image**
- Plain black footer: document title and page number only
- US Letter paper (12240 × 15840 DXA); margins ~1080 DXA top/bottom, 1008 DXA left/right

### Branded Client Guides (Funding Instructions & Checklist)

## Quality Check Preference
Always review output for:
- Unresolved `[PLACEHOLDER]` fields
- Unresolved `{{ template }}` tokens
- Missing or incorrect pronoun usage (he/she)
- Singular/plural mismatch (individual vs. joint)
- Missing guardian clause when minor children present
- Correct co-trustee provisions (include only when co-trustee named)

## MA Statutory References (do not alter)
- Certification of Trust: M.G.L. c. 203E §1013; M.G.L. c. 184 §35 and §36
- Pour-Over Will: M.G.L. general will execution requirements
- All statutory language must be reproduced verbatim — never paraphrase
