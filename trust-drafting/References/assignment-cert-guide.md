# Assignment of Personal Property & Certificate of Trust — Drafting Guide

Load this reference when drafting the Assignment of Personal Property
or the Certificate of Trust.

Source guide: `References/assignment-cert-guide.md`
Source templates: `Assets/Assignment of PP.docx`, `Assets/Certificate of Trust.docx`

---

## ASSIGNMENT OF PERSONAL PROPERTY

### Document Identity

- **Page size:** US Letter (12240 × 15840 DXA)
- **Margins:** Top 1080, Bottom 1080, Left 1008, Right 1008 DXA
- **Font:** Garamond throughout, 12pt, black `000000` only
- **Header:** None — no logo, no header image
- **Footer:** Plain black text — see Footer Table below

### When to Draft

- Individual trust / single client: **one document** for the client only.
- Married couple / Joint trust: **one document per person** (two total).
  Do NOT combine into a single document.

### File Naming

- Individual: `YYYY-MM-DD_Assignment-of-Personal-Property_[CLIENT-LAST-NAME].docx`
- Married:
  - `YYYY-MM-DD_Assignment-of-Personal-Property_[CLIENT-FULL-NAME].docx`
  - `YYYY-MM-DD_Assignment-of-Personal-Property_[SPOUSE-FULL-NAME].docx`

### Title

`ASSIGNMENT OF PERSONAL PROPERTY` — centered, Garamond 14pt bold, black `000000`.

### Opening Paragraph

```
For value received I, [CLIENT FULL NAME] of [CLIENT ADDRESS], assign,
transfer, and convey to:
```

### Trustee Block (vary by scenario)

- Joint Trust, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] and [SPOUSE FULL NAME] as Trustees, or their successors in
  interest of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto`

- Joint Trust, with co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME], [SPOUSE FULL NAME], and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] as Trustees,
  or their successors in interest of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any
  amendments thereto`

- Individual Trust, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] as Trustee, or their successor in interest of the [TRUST NAME]
  dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto`

- Individual Trust, with co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] as Trustees, or their successors in
  interest of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto`

### Property Description (verbatim — do not alter)

```
all of my right, title, and interest in all of my tangible personal property.
My tangible personal property includes all of my jewelry, clothing, household
furniture, furnishings and fixtures, chinaware, silver, photographs, works of art,
books, sporting goods, electronic equipment, musical instruments, artifacts relating
to my hobbies, and all other tangible articles of personal property that I now own
or later acquire, regardless of how they are acquired or the record title in which
they are held.
```

### Date and Signature Block

```
        Dated:  [DocDate]



                    [CLIENT FULL NAME], Assignor
```

Right-indented. Blank signature line above name (underscored line).

### Notary Block

One notary block per document (for the assignor named in this document):

```
    COMMONWEALTH OF MASSACHUSETTS

COUNTY OF [SIGNING COUNTY]

On [DocDate], before me, the undersigned notary public, personally appeared
[CLIENT FULL NAME], ___ personally known to me, or ___ proved to me through
satisfactory evidence of identification, which was a government-issued photo
identification to be the person whose name is signed on the preceding or attached
document, and who acknowledged that [he/she] signed it voluntarily for its stated
purpose.

WITNESS my hand and notarial seal.

(SEAL)

______________________________________

Notary Public

My Commission Expires: [NotaryExpiration]
```

Use correct pronoun: **he** (male) / **she** (female) for the individual assignor.

## CERTIFICATE OF TRUST

### Document Identity

- **One certificate per matter** (not per person, not per spouse)
- **File name:** `YYYY-MM-DD_Certificate-of-Trust_[CLIENT-LAST-NAME].docx`
- **Page size:** US Letter (12240 × 15840 DXA)
- **Margins:** Top 1080, Bottom 1080, Left 1008, Right 1008 DXA
- **Font:** Garamond throughout, 12pt, black `000000` only
- **Header:** None — no logo, no header image
- **Footer:** Plain black text, document title and page number

### Title Block

```
CERTIFICATION OF TRUST
Pursuant to M.G.L. Chapter 203E §1013 and Chapter 184 §35
```

Centered, Garamond 14pt bold, black `000000`.

### Opening Declaration (verbatim)

```
The undersigned hereby declare the following to be true and correct:

The [TRUST NAME] is currently in existence and was executed on [DocDate].
```

### Grantor Line (varies)

- Joint Trust: `The Grantors are [CLIENT FULL NAME] and [SPOUSE FULL NAME].`
- Individual Trust: `The Grantor is [CLIENT FULL NAME].`

### Trustee Line (varies)

- Joint Trust, no co-trustee:
  `The current trustees are [CLIENT FULL NAME] and [SPOUSE FULL NAME] of [Address].`
- Joint Trust, with co-trustee:
  `The current trustees are [CLIENT FULL NAME], [SPOUSE FULL NAME], and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] of [Address].`
- Individual Trust, no co-trustee:
  `The current trustee is [CLIENT FULL NAME] of [Address].`
- Individual Trust, with co-trustee:
  `The current trustees are [CLIENT FULL NAME] and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] of [Address].`

### Powers Paragraph (verbatim)

```
The powers of the Trustees include the power to acquire, sell, assign, convey,
pledge, encumber, lease, borrow, manage and deal with all real and personal
property interests held in the name of the trust.

Excerpts from the trust instrument that establish the trust, designate the Trustee
and set forth the powers of the Trustee will be provided upon request.
```

### Revocability Line (varies)

- Joint Trust: `The trust is revocable and may be revoked by the Grantors.`
- Individual Trust: `The trust is revocable and may be revoked by the Grantor.`

### Continuing Statutory Paragraphs (verbatim — do not alter)

```
There are no facts which constitute conditions precedent to acts by the Trustee or
which are in any other manner germane to the affairs of the Trust.

The trust is in full force and effect and has not been revoked, terminated or
otherwise in any manner which would cause the representations in this certification
of trust to be incorrect.
```

### Multiple-Trustee Paragraph (include ONLY if 2+ trustees)

```
The trust does have multiple trustees. The signature of one of the trustees is
required to exercise the powers of the trust.
```

**Omit entirely** for Individual Trust with no co-trustee.

### Titling Paragraph (verbatim intro, then varies)

```
Title to trust assets should be taken in the following fashion:
```

Bulleted titling format:
- Joint, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] and [SPOUSE FULL NAME] as Trustees, or their successor in
  interest, of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto.`

- Joint, with co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME], [SPOUSE FULL NAME], and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] as Trustees,
  or their successor in interest, of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any
  amendments thereto.`

- Individual, no co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] as Trustee, or his/her successor in interest, of the
  [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto.`

- Individual, with co-trustee:
  `[CLIENT FULL NAME] and [CO-TRUSTEE FULL NAME] as Trustees, or their successor in
  interest, of the [TRUST NAME] dated [DocDate], and any amendments thereto.`

### Titling Note (verbatim)

```
For titling purposes, any description referring to the trust is effective if it
includes the name of the trust, the name of at least one trustee, and any reference
indicating property is being held by the trustee in a fiduciary capacity.
```

### Non-Revocation Declaration (verbatim)

```
The undersigned trustees declare that the trust has not been revoked, modified, or
amended in any manner which would cause the representations contained in this
certification to be incorrect.
```

### Four Statutory Boilerplate Paragraphs (verbatim — M.G.L. citations)

Reproduce all four paragraphs exactly as they appear in the source guide.
Cite: M.G.L. c. 203E §1013(b), §1013(f), §1013(g), §1013(h).

### Execution Paragraph (verbatim, varies)

- Multiple trustees:
  ```
  This certification is being signed by all of the currently acting trustees and
  is being executed in conformity with the provisions of M. G. L. c. 203E, §1013
  and M. G. L. c. 184, §36.
  ```

- Single trustee only:
  ```
  This certification is being signed by the currently acting trustee and is being
  executed in conformity with the provisions of M. G. L. c. 203E, §1013 and
  M. G. L. c. 184, §36.
  ```

### Perjury Statement

`Signed under the pains and penalties of perjury on [DocDate].`

### Signature Blocks (one per trustee)

```
___________________________________
[TRUSTEE FULL NAME], Trustee
```

### Notary Blocks (one per trustee signatory)

Use same notary format as Assignment of Personal Property above.
Use correct pronoun (he/she) for each trustee.

| Joint | Yes | Plural | Plural | 3 | 3 | Yes |

| Individual | Yes | Singular | Plural | 2 | 2 | Yes |
